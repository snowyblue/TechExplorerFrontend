My name is Cody Tope and I am the founder of Tech Explorer.

This is my wonderful wife, Megan, and my beautiful baby daughter, Elli. I love them so much!

We live in rural northeastern Ohio with our 4 cats, 2 dogs, and 2 chickens. I earned my Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering from The Ohio State University in 2015 and earned my Master's in Business Administration from the University of Cincinnati in 2021. I am 30 years old and have spent time working as a design engineer, manufacturing engineer, quality manager, and manufacturing manager for several companies since graduating. I'm a DIYer at home and I have an infatuation with technology and learning how things work. I'm also a huge football enthusiast, go Bucks & Browns!

I created Tech Explorer, not to rehash the millions of crash courses available online, but as a way to share the resources I used to learn and to share my hands on projects and learning experiences with other tech enthusiasts and young engineers. I learn a lot of things by trial and error, so I screw up a LOT. These projects are mostly learn as you go, so don't be surprised when I do things wrong. None of these projects are by any means "cutting edge technology", but serve more to learn foundational skills in areas where I know little to nothing. At the end of the day, I just want to create a supportive community where we can all learn about technology together.
